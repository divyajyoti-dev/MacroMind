Layer 6 — Macro-Aware Reranker (V3 & V4)
src/reranker.py → rerank(). Multi-objective scoring with hard dietary violation penalty (−999). Uses difflib fuzzy matching for ingredient overlap. SearchResult[20] → RankedResult[5].
score = 1.0 − (0.5 × MAPEmacros + 0.3 × budget_overshoot + 0.2 × waste_fraction)
